# Predictive Model Report: Gilgit-Baltistan Assembly Elections 2026

## Introduction

This report provides a comprehensive predictive analysis for the 2026 Gilgit-Baltistan (GB) Assembly elections. Building upon initial models and integrating deep-dive research into social media sentiments, voter behavior, local/national news, and religious dynamics, this updated report offers a refined outlook on all 24 constituencies.

## Methodology

1.  **Initial Model Analysis**: Review of the `GB_2026_REVISED_Predictions_28May.xlsx` file.
2.  **Web Search and Deep Research**: Extensive searches on GB political landscape, social media sentiments (Facebook & X), and voter behavior.
3.  **Religious and Sectarian Dynamics**: Analysis of Shia, Sunni, and Ismaili population distributions and the influence of religious organizations.
4.  **Constituency-Level Deep Dive**: Nuanced analysis of local dynamics and candidate viability for all 24 seats.

## Conclusion

The 2026 elections signal a major shift toward the PPP, driven by strong ground organization and the collapse of the IPP. The PTI's base remains a potent but fragmented force due to the symbol ban, while PML-N maintains its strongholds in Diamer and parts of Astore.
